Operation Description
1. Hand 5cm close the sensitive switch, LED light will sensible flash and switch on the light, Hand close again, light off.
2. Press the push-switch once time, LED light lighting as normal mode. Hand close to turn on the light, second close to turn off the light.
3. Press the push-switch once time, LED little lighting, as delay light-off mode. Light lighting when hand close. Second hand close, the LED will lighting from slowly to quickly in 30 seconds, then light off.

Assembly Direction
1. Turn off the AC power source, and open the switch cases. Take off the wire, please clear up the copper wire if oxidized.
2. Put on one original line to sensitive switch hole 1 or 2, another line on hole 3-4.
3. Original switch as drawing 1 and change to sensitive switch as drawing 2.

注意事項
1. Please turn off the AC power source before assembly, avoid electric shock.
2. Please take note product voltage and load specification.
3. Avoid directly sun-shine and wet.
4. Product will enter the normal mode after AC power source on.
5. A little brand save power lamps have different electronic circuit design, will cause noncommittal problem (flash). Please add a 0.1uF 400V polyester film capacitors parallel connection the lamps.
