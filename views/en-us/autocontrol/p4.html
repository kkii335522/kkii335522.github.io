Product Picture

Operation Description
1. Continued handclap twice time turn on / off the light. The little curve palm to clap will make voice louder. 2 voice should between 0.2-0.5 second.
2. Press the push-switch once time, hand control the light on or off.
3. Press the push-switch over 3 seconds will start (or cancel) the handclap function. LED little light means off the handclap function, and LED high light means on the handclap function.

Assembly Direction
1. Turn off the AC power source, and open the switch cases. Take off the wire, please clear up the copper wire if oxidized.
2. Put on one original line to sensitive switch hole 1 or 2, another line on hole 3-4.
3. Original switch as drawing 1 and change to sensitive switch as drawing 2.

Notice
1. Please turn off the AC power source before assembly, avoid electric shock.
2. Please take note product voltage and load specification.
3. Avoid directly sun-shine and wet.
4. Product will enter the handclap voice function after power source on.
5. This product suitable for quite area. Noisily environment will wrong instruction of this control system.
6. A little brand save power lamps have different electronic circuit design, will cause noncommittal problem (flash). Please add a 0.1uF 400V polyester film capacitors parallel connection the lamps.
